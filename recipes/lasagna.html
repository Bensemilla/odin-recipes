Lasagna

Description:

This recipe is a great take on the all time classic that is lasagna. It substitutes the often used ingredient ricotta cheese for a basic white sauce.

Ingredients

For the white sauce:

  • 4 table spoons butter
  • 2 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the red sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 250g ground beef
  • 250g ground pork
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 6 to 8 white mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 3 500ml packs of tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste

For assembling:

  • 1 pack oven ready lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
